Collected projects

Undergraduate thesis: "A Mixed Methods Analysis of Thomsonian Literature, 1825-1860"

A computational analysis (paired with selective close readings) of 59 medical texts, conducted in order to determine the "populist-ness" of a nineteenth century medical movement called "Thomsonianism." Employs PCA and Topic Modeling techniques.

Ecoacoustics Stage (MIT Press): Program notes and sound file

This sound anthology uses various methods of data sonification to explore the natural world. Created under the guidance of Matthew Burtner, it was presented at the EcoSono Festival of Environmental Music and Sound Art in Anchorage, Alaska, in 2017.

The Second Law: Paperback and Kindle editions

A stage play about Henry David Thoreau, Sigmund Freud, and the Second Law of Thermodynamics. The show was produced by UVA's Spectrum Theater in 2017, and all proceeds from book sales are donated to the Brain and Behavior Research Foundation.
