Collected projects

Undergraduate thesis: "A mixed Methods Analysis of Thomsonian Literature, 1825-1860"

A computational analysis (paired with selective close readings) of 59 medical texts, conducted in order to determine the "populist-ness" of a nineteenth century medical movement called "Thomsonianism." Employs PCA and Topic Modeling techniques.

Ecoacoustics Stage (MIT Press): "Program notes"

Pages 103-104. This sound anthology uses various methods of data sonification to explore the natural world. Created under the guidance of Matthew Burtner, it was presented at the EcoSono Festival of Environmental Music and Sound Art in Anchorage, Alaska, in 2017.
